# Project Yada

**Codename:** Yada
**Status:** Pre-development — planning & IP concepting phase
**Started:** March 2026
**Founder:** Robert Dixon (solo)

---

## What It Is

A browser-native, original-IP game platform competing with G123 (CTW Inc.). The platform delivers deep gacha RPGs with Western-designed original IP, playable instantly on any device with no download. Targets North American and Western European players.

Full platform scope:
- Player-facing: game portal/discovery, instant play, cross-device sync, community
- Publisher-facing: original IP game development (in-house) + third-party developer ecosystem
- Monetization: Gacha IAP + Battle Pass (primary), Cosmetics, Rewarded Ads (floor)

---

## Strategic Position

**One-sentence pitch:** Build what G123 structurally cannot — a western-native original-IP browser game platform with Genshin-quality content, Poki-level UX friction, and a Fair Play Pledge on gacha monetization.

**Positioning statement:** For Western players who love deep RPGs but are tired of predatory gacha and download-heavy apps, Yada is the first browser-native original-IP game platform that delivers high-quality gacha RPGs with fair, transparent monetization — playable instantly on any device, owned by no one's license agreement.

---

## Target Market

- **Primary:** North America (US/Canada)
- **Secondary:** Western Europe (UK, Germany, France, Netherlands, Nordics)
- **Player profile:** 18-35 year olds who play gacha/RPG games on mobile, familiar with games like Genshin Impact, Honkai: Star Rail, Fate/Grand Order, but frustrated by download friction and P2W mechanics

| Kongregate | Dead platform. Proves the need exists but shows the execution gap. |

---

## Monetization Architecture

1. **Gacha banners** (primary) — limited-time character/equipment pulls
2. **Battle Pass** (~$9.99/mo) — conversion engine, monthly recurring
3. **Cosmetics** — skins, voice packs, themes (high-margin, non-P2W)
4. **Rewarded ads** — floor revenue from non-spending players

**Fair Play Pledge commitments:**
- Hard pity at 90 pulls (industry standard)
- Guaranteed rate-up or equivalent (50/50 mechanic)
- Fully disclosed drop rates
- All content completable F2P (spending = cosmetics + speed)
