## Key Strategic Pillars

1. **Original IP** — Own the characters and worlds outright. No licensing risk.
2. **Western identity** — Art style, cultural references, and content designed for Western players.
3. **Fair Play Pledge** — Ethical gacha: transparent odds, pity systems, F2P-completable content.
4. **Browser-first** — Zero friction, instant play, cross-device sync.
5. **Developer platform** — SDK and rev-share program to grow catalog without 100% internal dev.
