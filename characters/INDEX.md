## SOUL.md Structure

Each SOUL.md file contains these sections:
1. **Core Identity** — Name, archetype, personality reference, role, rarity
2. **What Makes Them Different** — Narrative hook, design philosophy
3. **Visual Identity** — Aesthetic, colors, features, silhouette rules, "what NOT to be"
4. **Voice & Dialogue** — Speaking style, signature phrases, red-line prohibitions
5. **Role in World** — Origin story, motivations, obstacles, arc direction
6. **Mystery Structure** — 10-chapter revelation pacing (story engagement spine)
7. **Appeal Register** — Psychological design anchor (why players want them)
8. **Relationships** — How they relate to other characters
9. **Combat Cards** — 6 card abilities (3 base + signature + 2 Depth unlocks)
10. **Content Generation Notes** — Instructions for AI dialogue & image generation
